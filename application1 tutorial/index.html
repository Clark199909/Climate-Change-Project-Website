Earth Orbit Application Tutorial

1

This is what you will see when you access the application. The application should have already been started. This page shows a sketch map of earth's orbit of rotation and its parameters.

2

You can click on different objects on this page to see more detailed descriptions. The places that you can click on are showing in the picture above. You can click on the small arrow icon on the bottom right corner to go to the next page.

3

After click that icon, you will see this page which shows a comparison of the graphs using the three orbital parameters and the insolation (solar radiation at 65 N in June) data. If you want to check the last page again, you can click the arrow icon again.

4

There are two bars on the right, which can be used to change the time range of the four graphs. You can click on locations on the bars to change the start year and the end year of the time range you want to see.
